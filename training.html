Salute Binding

First tell the recruit(s) about the /me command.

The /me command is used for roleplay purposes for any action you are committing.

Proceed to show the recruit(s) your salute bind.


Explain how to bind the salute action to a key.

We are now going to bind the salute key I showed you.
You will be required to salute often and it is required to be binded.
First, open the console. To open the console press the button under ESC on your keyboard.
Then copy this command onto the console input (white box).
bind p “say /me Salutes; act salute”
Fill free to replace p with the key you want the bind the salute button to.
Hit Enter on your keyboard or click the Submit button.
Press the key you binded salute to so I can confirm you have done so.

Faces

Go over the difference faces we have.

Questions to Ask

You must ask at least 4 questions from the question pool.

  • What are the Three DO NOTs of the server?
    Answer: DO NOT failRP, rdm, and spam.
  • What is the First Rule of Engagement (ROE)?
    Answer: Do not shoot at anyone unless it's war, authorized kidnapping, claimed area, raid, and never shoot your teamates unless it's a simulation.
  • What do you need to request in order to leave base when you are under the rank of Cpl & not during wartime?
    Answer: Request Permission to Leave (PTL) or /comms PTL : [reason].
  • When are you required to salute?
    Answer: When you or someone else gets promoted or when you see higher ranking officers.
  • What facility do you go to right before & after a war, when an advert is called?
    Answer: North Debrief.
  • What do you do in seperate/marine debrief?
    Answer: How marines did during war, Promotions, Tryout Info.
